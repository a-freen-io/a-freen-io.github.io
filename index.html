About

Who Am I

I’m a Computer Engineering student specialising in Artificial Intelligence at Vistula University, Warsaw. I build things - perception pipelines, web backends, arcade games, and everything in between. My work spans the full stack: from low-level deep learning models to clean REST APIs to organisational systems that actually work.

My flagship AI project is a multi-camera person re-identification pipeline - YOLOv8 detection, ByteTrack tracking, OSNet & CLIP embeddings - all built from scratch. But I’m just as comfortable shipping a Spring Boot API or debugging a Pygame physics bug at 2am.

Beyond the code, I’ve judged robotics competitions at national and international level in Kuwait and Turkey, completed an Erasmus+ exchange focused on cybersecurity and networks, and studied Mandarin in China. I bring a global perspective and the discipline to work across very different domains.
